# Foundation

## Purpose
This document is the "Bible" of the Gourmetica Platform. It establishes the core emotional intent, product principles, and philosophical foundation for every design and engineering decision.

## 1. Product Principles
Every implementation decision must satisfy these 10 principles:
1. **Clarity over decoration.**
2. **Typography before imagery.**
3. **Whitespace is a design element.**
4. **Motion should reinforce understanding, never distract.**
5. **Consistency beats creativity.**
6. **Accessibility is mandatory.**
7. **Performance is part of design.**
8. **Every component must have one clear responsibility.**
9. **Reuse before creating.**
10. **Every pixel must have a reason to exist.**

## 2. Who We Are
The Gourmetica Platform represents the pinnacle of hospitality marketing. We are the digital equivalent of a Michelin-starred experience: frictionless, elegant, deeply intentional, and devoid of clutter.

## 3. Who We Design For
We design for high-end hospitality brands, luxury dining establishments, and ambitious restaurant groups. They expect a digital experience that reflects their own standard of service.

## 4. Emotional Intent
The website must create feelings of:
- **Trust & Competence:** Achieved through flawless typography and strict grids.
- **Warmth & Hospitality:** Achieved through curated imagery and editorial pacing.
- **Effortless Luxury:** Achieved through butter-smooth, restrained motion and generous whitespace.

## 5. Why the Details Matter
- **Spacing:** Spacing is not just breathing room; it dictates pacing. It tells the user what to read and when.
- **Typography:** Oversized, confident typography commands attention and acts as our primary art direction.
- **Motion:** Restrained motion ensures we never feel like a cheap template. Every animation must disappear the moment it completes its job.
