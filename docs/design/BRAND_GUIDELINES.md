# Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Personality
This personality dictates every design choice.

### We ARE:
- Premium
- Confident
- Editorial
- Elegant
- Warm
- Hospitality-first
- Modern
- Minimal
- Timeless
- Human

### We are NOT:
- Playful
- Cyberpunk
- Neon
- Corporate
- Tech startup
- Glass overload
- Heavy gradients
- Flashy

## 2. Logo Usage
- The logo must always have adequate clear space.
- Never stretch, skew, or alter the proportions.
- Use the monochromatic white/black versions over busy backgrounds.

## 3. Tone of Voice
Our tone is confident, welcoming, and precise. We speak like a high-end concierge: knowledgeable but never arrogant.

- **Use:** "Discover our services."
- **Avoid:** "Click here to see what we do!!!"

## 4. Brand Colors
- **Primary Red:** `#E42528` (Used for primary actions and accents).
- **Black:** `#000000`
- **White:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Neutrals:** Derived luxuriously from black/white blending. No random grays.
