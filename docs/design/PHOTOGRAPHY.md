# Photography Guidelines

This document establishes the strict rules for imagery used across the Gourmetica Platform.

## 1. Allowed Imagery
Photography should convey a premium, human, and authentic hospitality experience.
- Luxury dining and plated food.
- Warm, natural lighting (especially golden hour).
- Authentic chef moments (plating, preparing).
- Guests interacting naturally.
- Restaurant interiors showing atmosphere and depth.
- Shallow depth of field to draw focus.
- Editorial compositions.

## 2. Not Allowed (Prohibited Imagery)
We absolutely avoid imagery that looks cheap, corporate, or artificial.
- ❌ Office people or business suits.
- ❌ Handshake photos.
- ❌ Stock smiling teams looking at the camera.
- ❌ Blue, cold, corporate lighting.
- ❌ Fake marketing photography.
- ❌ AI-generated looking images.
- ❌ Cluttered or messy scenes.

## 3. Implementation in UI
- Imagery should rarely be full brightness if text is overlaid. Use consistent overlays or gradients.
- Images should be optimized via `next/image` to prevent layout shifts and ensure rapid loading.
